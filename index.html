Accurate Thermometer

Description:

A microbit is used to calculate the average temperature of a room or environment.

How it works:

It works by taking 6 temperatures, one every 5 minutes. It then divides the results to give you the average temperature of a room in the last 30 minutes. The system will let you know when it has taken a temperature and what temperature it was (i.e. Temp 1 = 34, Temp 2 = 22, Temp 3 = 34, etc.) and it will then give you the average temperature after the 6th temperature take.
